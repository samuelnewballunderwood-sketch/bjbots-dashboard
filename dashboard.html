⚙️ Manage Bots
✅ 3Commas Bots — Fully Automatic
Any bot you create or delete in 3Commas will automatically appear or disappear here on the next dashboard refresh. Capital, direction, and strategy are all read directly from the 3Commas API. You don't need to do anything.
⚠️ Binance Native Bots — One-Time Setup
Binance's API does not have an endpoint that lists your active Grid or DCA bots — this is a known limitation of the Binance API that even professional platforms cannot work around. You need to register each Binance bot once using the form below. After that, trade counts, scores and risk calculations update automatically.
📋 How to register a Binance bot — step by step
1. Open Binance
Go to Binance → Trading Bots → find your active bot
2. Find the details
Note the trading pair (e.g. BTC/USDT), how much capital you put in, and whether it's Grid or DCA
3. Fill in the form
Enter those details below. The symbol should be in format BTCUSDT (no slash)
4. What happens next
A card appears instantly. AlphaControl will track its trades, score its performance, and include it in your risk calculations — all automatically from then on
➕ Register a Binance Bot
Fill in the details exactly as they appear in your Binance Trading Bots page. Capital is the total amount you allocated to this bot when you set it up (e.g. $400 for a grid bot). This is stored in your browser and never sent anywhere.
